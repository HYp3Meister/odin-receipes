Lasagna

This unique lasagna is filled with artichokes and spinach and topped with crumbled herb and garlic feta cheese. Reviewers say you don't need to boil your lasagna noodles before cooking, instead, you can use no-boil noodles or dry noodles that will soften from the hot sauce.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg
