Pizza Miaaa

This simple brick-oven pizza recipe has been made famous by several well-known, Brooklyn wood-fire pizzerias. Best accompanied by ice-cold, pilsner-style lager beer. Ingredients

Dough:

  • 1/4 cup warm of water
  • 1 teaspoon active dry yeast
  • 1 cup cold water
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups bread flour
  • 1 tablespoon extra-virgin oil

Toppings:

  • 6 ounces low moisture mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil
  • 6 leaves fresh basil, torn
